About Us

The Arts Center at NYU Abu Dhabi is a cultural hub for Abu Dhabi and the UAE that hosts major events and arts programming throughout the year. The Arts Center provides facilities for theater, music, film, visual arts, and interactive media activation through its state-of-the-art facilities and professional production crew.

Mission & Vision

Showcasing music, theater, dance, film, poetry, family programs, and interdisciplinary performances that defy definition, The Arts Center at NYU Abu Dhabi presents distinguished professional artists from around the world alongside student, faculty, and community productions.
